Our professional events enable Phi Chi Theta Brothers to engage in professional development and networking. Through talking to Alumni, Professional Speakers and fellow brothers, they are able to understand the paths they wish to pursue for their career. We host alumni panels, professional speaker events, participate in firm visits and various other professional events.



Philanthropy

We strive to give back to the community by participating in philanthropy events such as food drives, soup kitchens, charity runs, environmental clean ups and many more!



Brotherhood

We are a professional fraternity, but that doesn’t mean we don’t like to have fun! With such a tight knit group, there are events and activities for all a wide variety of interests. We hold social events, ranging from paintballing to hiking to Halloween haunted forests.
